How to make a sandwich

Hi i'm Alexander. Welcome to how to make a sandwich. I'm going to teach you how to make a sandwich on this page.

The following are the steps you need to make a sandwich:

  1. Get a plate
  2. Get two pieces of bread
  3. Get a knife
  4. Get jelly and peanut butter
  5. Spread the jelly on one side and the peanut butter on the other
  6. Put the two pieces of bread together
  7. Put the sandwich on the plate
  8. Eat the sandwich
